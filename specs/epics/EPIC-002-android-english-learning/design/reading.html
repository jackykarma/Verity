Why We Need Sleep

Sleep is essential for our physical and mental health. During sleep, our bodies repair tissues, consolidate memories, and regulate emotions. Research shows that adults typically need seven to nine hours of sleep each night...

Lack of sleep can lead to impaired cognitive function, mood changes, and increased risk of chronic conditions. Many people underestimate the importance of good sleep habits.
